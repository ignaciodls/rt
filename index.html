How to clean IQOS

Your customer can use IQOS Cleaning Sticks to keep their IQOS device clean from any residue, and get the true taste of HEETS tobacco.

1.

There’s no liquid involved in the cleaning process. Once the holder has cooled, the cap is slid off.

2.

The holder is then tapped to release any loose tobacco inside it. The cleaning tool is inserted and twisted 2 or 3 times.

3.

Then, the cleaning stick is used to clean the inside of the holder and the cap, top and bottom.

4.

IQOS is clean from any residue.
